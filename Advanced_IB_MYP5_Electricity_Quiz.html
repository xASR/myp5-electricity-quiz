Task 1: Multi-Charge Force Interactions (Criterion A)

Two identical point charges q = 3.0 × 10⁻⁶ C are placed 0.2 m apart in air. A third charge Q = -6.0 × 10⁻⁶ C is placed exactly midway between them.

Q1: Calculate the net electrostatic force experienced by charge Q. Use vector analysis. Show all steps. Use: k = 9.0 × 10⁹ Nm²/C²

Task 2: High-Load Circuit Engineering (Criterion B)

You are tasked with designing a resistive heating system that draws a total power of 1.5 kW from a 240V mains supply.

Q2:

  • a) Should the 40Ω heaters be connected in series or parallel to meet the requirement? Justify with calculations.
  • b) Calculate the total current drawn from the supply.
  • c) Calculate the power consumed by each resistor.

Task 3: Circuit Analysis with Internal Resistance (Criterion C)

A 12V battery with internal resistance r = 1.5Ω is connected to an external resistor R = 10.5Ω.

  • Q3a: Calculate the current in the circuit.
  • Q3b: Calculate the terminal voltage across the external resistor.
  • Q3c: Calculate the percentage of energy lost to internal resistance over 10 minutes.
